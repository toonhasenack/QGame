Please fill in your names below to start playing Quantum Tic Tac Toe. The idea is to entangle two simultaneous games of tic tac toe and try to win on either of the boards.
You have to pay attention, however, because you only know with 50% certainty which state will be picked.
So choose carefully such that your opponent does not win because of your move!
